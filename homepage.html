Introduction to Ayurveda and Prakriti

Ayurveda is a natural healthcare system that emphasizes personalized treatment based on an individual's unique constitution known as Prakriti.

Prakriti classification is based on the theory of Tridosha.

Importance of Prakriti Assessment

Understanding one's Prakriti is essential in Ayurveda as it enables personalized healthcare and disease prevention.

Prakriti assessment helps individuals make informed dietary and lifestyle choices.

Features of Self-Assessment Tool

The self-assessment tool simplifies the Prakriti assessment process by providing a convenient way for individuals to assess their Prakriti online.

It utilizes artificial intelligence and a pre-recorded questionnaire.

Benefits of Prakriti Assessment

Knowing one's Prakriti aids in disease prevention, management, and overall well-being.

It allows individuals to tailor their lifestyle, including diet and activities, according to their unique constitution.
